Resources

With legal forms and financial information at your fingertips, you can better understand laws relating to personal and business matters in your own state. The Moda Health EAP gives you free online access to Cascade’s Legal and Financial Resource Library. This legal hub contains thousands of legal and financial forms, over 45 financial calculators, professionally written articles, FAQs and much more. You can even create legal documents free of charge using your own personal account.

Visit Cascade’s Legal and Financial Resource Library to access free, fillable legal forms and other financial documents, including:

Personal legal documents

  • Living trust
  • Complaint letters
  • Family and divorce

Business legal documents

  • Business agreements
  • Corporations and LLCs
  • Intellectual property
  • Loans and collections

Legal information

  • Consumer finance and protection
  • Credit, debt collection, identity theft
  • Bankruptcy
  • Civil rights
